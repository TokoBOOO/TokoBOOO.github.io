Why Donate Your Books?

Expand Access

Books in public libraries remove financial barriers to reading.

Support Literacy

Donations enrich after-school and adult education programs.

Eco-Friendly

Reuse reduces waste and conserves resources.

Community Events

Funds storytelling hours, workshops, and cultural gatherings.

Benefits of Donating

  • Tax Deductions: Official receipts available at most libraries.
  • Declutter: Free up space with purpose.
  • Learning Impact: Support diverse educational resources.
  • Global Perspectives: Share books in multiple languages.

How to Prepare Your Books

  • Ensure covers and pages are clean and intact.
  • Remove personal annotations and bookmarks.
  • Sort by genre or age group.
  • Package in sturdy boxes or reusable bags.
  • Label contents for easy library sorting.

Donation Steps

  • Find a Library: Check local directories or online maps.
  • Read Policies: Review accepted materials list.
  • Drop Off: Visit during donation hours.
  • Get Receipt: Ask for documentation.
  • Volunteer: Join workshops or book drives.
