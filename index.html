Why Donating Books Matters

Passing on your gently loved books brings fresh stories to new readers. It fuels imagination, supports education, and strengthens communities worldwide.

Learn More

Why Donate Your Books?

Equal Access

Libraries free readers from cost barriers, offering knowledge to all.

Boost Literacy

Donations enrich school and adult programs, sparking lifelong learning.

Sustainable

Re-circulate books and reduce environmental impact of new printing.

Cultural Exchange

Share diverse voices and perspectives with global readers.

Benefits of Donating

  • Tax Receipt: Get an official acknowledgment for deductions.
  • Declutter: Clear your shelves for fresh finds.
  • Community: Strengthen local programs and events.
  • Fulfillment: Feel the joy of giving back.

Preparing Your Books

  • Ensure covers and pages are clean, no severe damage.
  • Remove personal notes, bookmarks, or sticky tabs.
  • Sort by genre or reader age for quick library processing.
  • Pack in sturdy boxes or reusable totes.
  • Label contents to assist library volunteers.
